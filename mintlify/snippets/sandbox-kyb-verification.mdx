In sandbox, you can trigger specific KYC/KYB verification outcomes using magic suffixes in customer and beneficial owner fields. These let you test different verification flows without waiting for real review.

### Business customer verification

The **last 3 digits** of the `registrationNumber` in `businessInfo` determine the KYB verification outcome when you call `POST /verifications`:

| Suffix | `verificationStatus` | Behavior |
|--------|---------------------|----------|
| **001** | `REJECTED` | KYB verification is rejected |
| **002** | `RESOLVE_ERRORS` | Returns errors for missing fields/documents |
| **Any other** | `APPROVED` | KYB verification is approved |

### Beneficial owner KYC

The **last 3 characters** of the `lastName` in `personalInfo` determine the individual KYC outcome:

| Suffix | `kycStatus` | Behavior |
|--------|------------|----------|
| **001** | `REJECTED` | KYC verification is rejected |
| **Any other** | `APPROVED` | KYC verification is approved |

### Document verification

The **last 3 characters** of the `documentNumber` determine the document verification outcome:

| Suffix | Result | Behavior |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **001** | `REJECTED` | Document is rejected (bad quality, mismatch, etc.) |
| **Any other** | `APPROVED` | Document is approved |
