Payment Rail Glossary
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Europe - SEPA">
    **SEPA (Single Euro Payments Area)** - Standard bank transfers across 36 European countries
**SEPA Instant** - Real-time transfers completed in under 10 seconds, available 24/7/365
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Americas">
    **PIX** (Brazil) - Instant payment system, 24/7 availability
**TED/DOC** (Brazil) - Traditional bank transfers

**SPEI** (Mexico) - Real-time electronic fund transfers

**ACH** (US) - Automated Clearing House, 1-2 business days

**RTP** (US) - Real-Time Payments network

**FedNow** (US) - Federal Reserve instant payment service

**EFT** (Canada) - Electronic Funds Transfer

**Interac** (Canada) - Instant e-Transfer system

**SINPE** (Costa Rica) - Instant payment system
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Asia-Pacific">
    **UPI/IMPS/NEFT/RTGS** (India) - Unified Payments Interface and various instant/standard transfer systems
**PromptPay** (Thailand) - Real-time payment system

**BAHTNET** (Thailand) - Interbank payment system

**PayNow** (Singapore) - Peer-to-peer and business payment system

**FAST** (Singapore) - Fast and Secure Transfers

**FPS** (Hong Kong) - Faster Payment System

**CHATS** (Hong Kong) - Clearing House Automated Transfer System

**DuitNow** (Malaysia) - Instant transfer service

**FPX** (Malaysia) - Financial Process Exchange

**InstaPay** (Philippines) - Real-time electronic fund transfer

**PESONet** (Philippines) - Batch electronic fund transfer

**BI-FAST** (Indonesia) - Bank Indonesia Fast Payment System

**VietQR** (Vietnam) - QR-based instant payment

**NAPAS** (Vietnam) - National Payment Corporation system

**Alipay/WeChat Pay/UnionPay** (China) - Major digital payment platforms
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Africa">
    **Mobile Money** - Widely used across Africa for instant transfers (M-Pesa in Kenya, MTN, Airtel, etc.)
**M-Pesa** (Kenya) - Leading mobile money platform

**GhIPSS Instant Pay** (Ghana) - Real-time payment system

**NIP** (Nigeria) - Nigeria Inter-Bank Settlement System Instant Payment

**RTC** (South Africa) - Real-Time Clearing

**ZIPIT** (Zambia) - Instant payment system
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Other Regions">
    **Vipps** (Norway) - Mobile payment application
**Swish** (Sweden) - Mobile payment system

**TWINT** (Switzerland) - Mobile payment solution

**SIC** (Switzerland) - Swiss Interbank Clearing

**SLIPS** (Sri Lanka) - Sri Lanka Interbank Payment System
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
