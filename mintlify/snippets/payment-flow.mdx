## Payment Flow
<Steps>
  <Step title="On-Ramp">
    Fund with fiat/crypto and convert to BTC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Lightning Settlement">
    Instantly send payment to the receiving locale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Off-Ramp & Payout">
    Convert BTC to local fiat for instant deposit via local banking rails.
</Steps>
