Global Accounts are powered by an embedded wallet primitive. Export lets a customer take the wallet credentials off Grid when your product supports that flow.

Export is a sensitive action. It requires customer authentication and a signed request.

## Export flow

At a high level:

1. The customer authenticates with a registered credential.
2. The client generates or selects a key for encrypted delivery.
3. Grid returns a challenge for the export action.
4. The customer signs the export payload.
5. Grid returns encrypted wallet credentials.
6. The client decrypts and handles the credentials according to your product's security requirements.

<Warning>
  Wallet export gives the customer control of credentials that can move funds. Design this flow carefully and avoid exposing secrets to your backend or logs.
</Warning>
